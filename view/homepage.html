Welcome to Quiz App


QuizBuzz is an innovative online quiz platform designed to enhance learning and assessment experiences for students, teachers, and administrators. It offers a dynamic environment where teachers can create and manage quizzes, students can participate in interactive tests, and admins can oversee the entire system efficiently. With features like real-time results, question randomization, detailed analytics, and bulk imports, QuizBuzz aims to make education engaging, personalized, and data-driven. Its user-friendly interface and role-based access ensure seamless navigation and secure usage for everyone involved.
